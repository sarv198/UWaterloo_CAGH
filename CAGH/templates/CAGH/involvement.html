Voices for Global Health Podcast

Join our student-led podcast that explores various global health disparities by focusing on social determinants of health. We take a population-focused approach to understanding global health issues!

Our podcast covers diverse topics including:

  • Forest fires and health impacts
  • Invisible disabilities awareness
  • Obesity and public health
  • Parental-child separation
  • Poverty and health outcomes
  • Lower back pain perspectives
  • Mental health advocacy
  • Environmental health issues

Get Involved: We're always looking for passionate students to join our podcast team as hosts, researchers, or contributors!
