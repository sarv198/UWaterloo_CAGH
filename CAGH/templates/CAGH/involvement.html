Get Involved with UW CAGH

Join our community of passionate individuals working to make a difference in global health. There are many ways to contribute, regardless of your background or experience level.

Ways to Get Involved

Become a Member

Join our growing community of students passionate about global health. Membership gives you access to:

  • Exclusive workshops and seminars
  • Networking opportunities with professionals
  • Priority registration for events
  • Access to research opportunities
  • Leadership development programs

Membership Fee: $20/year for students, $30/year for non-students

Volunteer Opportunities

Make a direct impact through our various volunteer programs:

Event Support
  • Event planning and coordination
  • Registration and check-in
  • Technical support
  • Photography and social media
Community Outreach
  • Health education programs
  • Community health fairs
  • Fundraising activities
  • Awareness campaigns

Time Commitment: Flexible - from 2 hours/month to 10+ hours/month based on your availability

Executive Positions
  • President & Vice President
  • Secretary & Treasurer

  • Communications Director
Committee Leads
  • Research & Education

  • Fundraising & Partnerships

Research Opportunities

  • Undergraduate research assistant positions
  • Independent research projects
